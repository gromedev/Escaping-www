Intune Device Compliance Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Device Shows Non-Compliant

Check the compliance policy assignments. Navigate to Intune > Devices > Compliance policies. Verify the policy is assigned to the correct group and the device is a member.

Compliance Check Not Running

Compliance evaluations run every 8 hours by default. Force a sync from the Company Portal app or the Intune portal. Check that the Intune Management Extension service is running on the device.

Conditional Access Blocking Despite Compliance

There can be a delay between the device becoming compliant and the CA policy recognizing it. Wait 15-30 minutes. Check the sign-in logs in Entra ID for the specific CA policy that's blocking.

BitLocker Compliance Failures

Ensure the BitLocker compliance setting matches the actual encryption state. Some devices report "Not applicable" if TPM is not available. Check the hardware hash in Autopilot.
